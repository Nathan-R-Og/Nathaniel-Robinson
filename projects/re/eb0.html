Names: Mother 1, Mother, Earthbound Beginnings, Earthbound Zero, Earth Bound...
Github Wikipedia MobyGames GameFAQS

I've always loved the Mother franchise, either for it's usage of the Role-Playing Game medium, or just it's style in general. I had always liked EarthBound, and when the Mother 3 fan translation officially completed, it was a stellar experience! I never hear anything about the first one, though...

In 2019, when I played it for the first time on the only official release (the Wii U virutal console), I genuinely had no idea what I was in for. I might as well be playing Dragon Quest at this point! Argh! Why does the grinding suck so bad! Argh! I'm so illiterate!, Needless to say, I didn't quite understand it. I enjoyed what I played, but it felt like more of a slog than a game.

Fast forward about 5 years later, I finally have reason to play it all again. This time, though. I allow myself to use a wiki, and there was SO MUCH I MISSED. From every enemy having a check description, to there being shops that just GIVE you armor... I thought I was dreaming! Where was this when I played!!!

That wake up call that told me "You played the game wrong!" reclocked my mind. After finishing this new and enlightened run, I got it. The game immediately shot up to my top 10, at LEAST.

So, right off the heels of Chalvo 55, what do you think my natural conclusion was?
Duh, to start a disassembly!

I had a bit of an NES template set up with Donkey Kong Jr. Math, mostly because I thought it'd be funny. However, this game takes SUCH a higher presidence.

As of now, the repository has about half of the main game data disassembled, with some left to go. I have barely touched the code since reading that this early without understanding the data types and structure would be a nightmare. I've also mainly focused on understanding where data is called and pointed at, to make mods and linking easier.

It's been very fun! The craziest part is half the stuff I found hasn't even been documented yet! You'd think on a game like this it'd be more prevelant, but nope! I get Chameleon Twist and Chalvo, since those are games with double digit populated fanbases, but this???? Come on man.
