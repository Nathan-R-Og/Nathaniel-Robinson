Itch.io

Run through an unknown world in a Metroidvania?-like to escape and never look back!

In a world left completely unexplained, try your hand at some tricky jumps and escape with your life. Meet some friendly (enough) faces and jump higher in the process. Short and simple, shouldn't take more than 10 minutes to complete.

Why?

I created this as a short game during my high school years to see if I had any amount of management skills to create and put something out as quick as possible. I'd like to think it worked, though I sat on it for a couple months dwelling on it, so I don't know how much it really counts.


Tools

For this project, I sketched characters in Medibang Paint Pro and did all spriting in Piskel. The whole thing was written in GDScript by myself. The music was originally going to be dumb ambient loops in FL Studio, but I had a producer friend help iron the kinks out.

Behind the Scenes

For a project that took less than a month of time to make, I put a good amount of thought behind the design of this silly little world.

It felt like I had known what I was doing before I got into it??? Despite everything just falling into place. Regardless of the outcome, I'm proud enough of it to show this stuff.
